Build a web experience called "DreamWalk".

Tagline:
"Walk inside your music."

DreamWalk is an atmospheric music experience inspired by the emotional feeling of Journey.

IMPORTANT:
This is NOT a game.
This is NOT a music visualizer.

It is a meditative, cinematic experience where users enter a beautiful world generated from a song.

--------------------------------------------------
VISUAL DIRECTION
--------------------------------------------------

Inspiration:
- Journey
- Sky: Children of the Light
- Monument Valley
- PlayStation Dreams

Core feelings:
- wonder
- solitude
- peace
- nostalgia
- discovery
- emotional connection

The world should feel:

- vast
- minimal
- dreamlike
- mysterious
- sacred

--------------------------------------------------
ART STYLE
--------------------------------------------------

No realistic graphics.

Use:

- stylized low poly
- large empty landscapes
- soft gradients
- atmospheric fog
- volumetric lighting
- floating particles
- distant silhouettes

Avoid:

- detailed assets
- photorealism
- complex textures
- cluttered environments

Everything should look like a playable painting.

--------------------------------------------------
COLOR PALETTES
--------------------------------------------------

Golden Desert
Warm orange, sand, sunset.

Moon Ocean
Deep blue, silver, stars.

Ancient Kingdom
Gold, white, clouds.

Emerald Valley
Green, teal, morning fog.

Snow Sanctuary
White, blue, aurora.

Dream Night
Purple, indigo, stars.

--------------------------------------------------
WORLD GENERATION
--------------------------------------------------

Generate landscapes using primitives:

- dunes
- mountains
- floating islands
- arches
- monoliths
- pillars
- ruins
- cloth banners

No buildings are necessary.

No NPCs.

No enemies.

No inventory.

--------------------------------------------------
CAMERA
--------------------------------------------------

The camera is extremely important.

Use:

- slow cinematic movement
- subtle drift
- smooth transitions
- gentle zoom
- large scale compositions

Camera should make the player feel small in a huge world.

--------------------------------------------------
MOVEMENT
--------------------------------------------------

The user can:

- walk slowly
- rotate camera
- stop and observe

Movement speed should be peaceful.

No running.

No jumping.

No combat.

--------------------------------------------------
MUSIC SYNCHRONIZATION
--------------------------------------------------

Visual changes should be subtle.

Verse:
- soft wind
- small particles

Chorus:
- stronger light
- banners move
- camera slightly rises

Bridge:
- color transition

Emotional climax:
- sun rays
- aurora
- particle bloom

Avoid aggressive visual effects.

The music should feel alive, not like a nightclub visualizer.

--------------------------------------------------
LYRICS TO WORLD
--------------------------------------------------

Use Musixmatch lyrics and keywords.

Examples:

"desert"
→ dunes and sand.

"stars"
→ night sky.

"ocean"
→ moonlit sea.

"mountain"
→ distant peaks.

"castle"
→ ruins and towers.

"fire"
→ floating embers.

"home"
→ warm lighting.

--------------------------------------------------
UI DESIGN
--------------------------------------------------

Minimal UI.

Inspired by Journey.

No sidebars.

No dashboard.

No complex menus.

Only:

- logo
- song title
- artist name
- small play button
- enter dream button

Inside the experience:

Hide all UI.

Only show:

- pause
- screenshot
- exit

Everything fades in and fades out.

--------------------------------------------------
TRANSITIONS
--------------------------------------------------

Transitions should feel magical.

Fade through fog.

Camera dives into clouds.

Sunlight bloom.

Floating particles.

--------------------------------------------------
EMOTIONAL GOAL
--------------------------------------------------

The user should feel:

"I am walking inside the emotion of this song."

Prioritize:

1. atmosphere
2. emotion
3. beauty
4. simplicity

Never prioritize gameplay.
Never prioritize realism.
Never prioritize complexity.

This is an emotional journey generated from music.
